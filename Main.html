Who we Are

We are Iceman Racing. Founded in 1992, this upcoming season will be our 30th in the Formula One™ World Championship. This is a team with both a rich heritage and a fresh perspective – bringing new energy to the sport with a determination to shake up the order and compete at the sharp end. We are all in, and everyone is welcome.




Meet Our Drivers

Kimi Räikkönen

Legendary Formula 1 Driver and 2007 World Champion Kimi Räikkönen leads the Iceman Racing Formula One™ Team determined to share his race-winning and title-winning experience as Formula One undergoes a technical revolution in 2022.

Sebastian Vettel

Four-time World Champion Sebastian Vettel completes the Iceman Racing Formula One™ Team driver line-up. This will be his 8th season with our team and is keen to defend the Drivers World Championship he won in 2021.
